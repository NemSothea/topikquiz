How it works: This app uses a Spaced Repetition System (SRS). Words you answer correctly will appear less frequently, while words you struggle with will appear more often.

Each word comes with a Korean example sentence to help you understand usage in context. Focus on understanding the highlighted word in the example. Words are grouped into 5 levels. Correct answers move words up a level, incorrect answers move them down. Level 5 words are considered "mastered".
